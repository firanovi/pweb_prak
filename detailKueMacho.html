Kue Macho is a traditional cake from Sumenep on Madura. This cake is known for its sweet taste and soft texture, and it is often sold as a typical souvenir from Madura. Besides being enjoyed as a daily snack, Kue Macho is also sometimes served at family gatherings, celebrations, or when welcoming guests.
