Sejarah

Located northeast of Java and part of East Java, Madura Island has a long history dating back to the Majapahit Kingdom as a center of shipping and salt trade in the archipelago.

The arrival of Islam, led by Islamic scholars and traders, shaped the Madurese people's religious, resilient, and tradition-honoring character.

To this day, this historical legacy lives on in the culture of bull racing, traditional cuisine, and beautiful tourist destinations like Gili Labak, which showcase Madura's charm to the world.
