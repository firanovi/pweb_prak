Batik Sumenep is a traditional batik craft originating from Sumenep in the region of Madura. This batik is known for its bright colors and distinctive motifs that reflect the culture and daily life of the Madurese people.

The motifs of Batik Sumenep are often inspired by nature, plants, and local cultural symbols, such as flowers, leaves, and geometric patterns. Common colors used include red, yellow, blue, and green, which symbolize the cheerfulness and bravery of the Madurese community.
